Instructions

Whenever you visit a grove, there are three bird species you might spot: bluebirds, greenbirds, and redbirds. On any one trip you may spot zero species, one species, or multiple species. Your goal is to spot as many bird species as you can on each trip.

Some bird species may tend to prefer a single grove, while others may appear randomly throughout the six groves in a forest. Some species may appear more often in some forests than in other forests. The three species ignore each other, so the presence of one bird species won't make another one more or less likely to appear.

Each time you choose a grove, the "trips in forest" counter will decrease by one, the birds you've spotted will be displayed below the grove icons, and the "birds spotted" counter will increase by the number of species spotted on that trip. The "trips in forest" and "birds spotted" counters will reset on each expedition to a new forest. At the end of the game, one of your expeditions will be chosen at random and you will receive a cash bonus based on the number of total birds spotted on that expedition. The birds spotted are converted to money at a rate of one cent per bird.
