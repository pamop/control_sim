Instructions

When you make a trip into a cave, you will find some number of emeralds and some number of sapphires. Some caves may have more emeralds than others, and some caves may have more sapphires than others.

Each time you take a trip into a cave, the number of emeralds and sapphires you find there might be a little different. However, the average number of emeralds and sapphires found in each of the caves doesn't change over the course of an expedition, and caves don't "run out" of gems.

Your goal is to mine the most total gems on each expedition. Throughout the expedition, the game will keep track of how many gems you've mined. Emeralds and sapphires are counted equally—each counts as one gem towards your total.

At the end of the game, one of your expeditions will be chosen at random and you will receive a cash bonus based on your gem total for that expedition. The gems are converted to money at a rate of 20 gems to 1 cent.
